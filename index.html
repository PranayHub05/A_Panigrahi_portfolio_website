About

I am currently pursuing a B.A. (Hons) in Bengali at Presidency University, Kolkata. My work sits at the intersection of literary history, performance traditions, and everyday cultural practices.

I am particularly interested in how texts travel across time — through oral performance, manuscript circulation, and contemporary reinterpretations — and how language encodes the social worlds that produce it.

Core research interests

  • Medieval Bengali literature
  • Folklore and oral traditions
  • Historical linguistics
  • Theory of drama

This site is a living archive of ongoing projects, notes, and experiments in reading Bengali texts closely and slowly.
